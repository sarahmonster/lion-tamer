Reading is an adventure. Come explore with us!

Suitable for ages 4–8, Reading Safari is a new program to promote literacy and a love of learning in a fun, educational environment. For an hour, Reading Safari leaders will guide children through the magical world of books, promoting interaction, questioning, and storytelling. Along the way, we’ll encounter magical princesses, fantastical beasts, and all kinds of adventures!

Autumn program

Our autumn program will run from October 11th to November 22nd.

Every Saturday afternoon from 3 to 4pm, we’ll be at the Elmvale Library, exploring new worlds through literature.
